Quick answers

What's a typical salon commission split?

Most commission salons pay somewhere between 30% and 60% of the service price, depending on experience, the salon's prices and what's included (products, marketing, front desk). Always ask: percentage of what?

Do tips get split with the salon?

Normally no — tips are yours. This calculator adds the tip on top of your cut, after the split.

Is anything I type saved?

No. There's no signup, no tracking of your numbers — the math happens on your device and stays there.
