Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026  ·  CommissionPro by Shahbaz Mirshahi

✓ We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. Your appointment records belong to you.

1. Who we are

CommissionPro is operated by Shahbaz Mirshahi ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and App at shaz010.github.io/mgm-studio.

2. What information we collect

When you sign up:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your salon name
  • Your commission rate
  • Your phone number (optional)

When you use the App:

  • Client names you enter
  • Appointment dates and times
  • Service charges and tip amounts
  • Service categories/notes

We do not collect:

  • Payment card details (handled entirely by Stripe)
  • Your location
  • Browser history or tracking cookies
  • Any data beyond what you explicitly enter

3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Set up your personal CommissionPro instance
  • Contact you regarding your setup and account
  • Provide customer support if you contact us
  • Send you important updates about the App (rarely)

We will never use your information for marketing, advertising, or share it with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Where your data is stored

Your appointment data is stored securely in a private Supabase database hosted on AWS (Amazon Web Services) in the United States. Supabase uses industry-standard encryption for data in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest.

Your signup information is processed via Formspree and delivered to our secure email. We do not retain your signup data in any additional database.

Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your payment card details.

5. Data retention

Your appointment data is retained in your private database for as long as your account is active. If you wish to have your data deleted, contact us at chabokchamejun@gmail.com and we will delete it within 7 business days.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request we correct any inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request we delete your data
  • Portability — request your data in a readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at chabokchamejun@gmail.com.

7. Third-party services

CommissionPro uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database hosting: supabase.com/privacy
  • Stripe — payment processing: stripe.com/privacy
  • Formspree — signup form: formspree.io/privacy
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting: github.com/privacy

8. Children's privacy

CommissionPro is intended for professional use by adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18 years of age.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect the latest update. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
