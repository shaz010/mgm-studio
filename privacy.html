Privacy Policy

Who we are

Your Salon is operated by Shahbaz Mirshahi. Questions? chabokchamejun@gmail.com

What data we collect

Your Salon collects only what it needs to run:

  • Account: your email address and password (via Firebase Authentication)
  • Salon data: appointment records, client names and phone numbers, earnings, commission splits, and service details you enter
  • Preferences: theme, colour, and speed settings stored locally on your device
  • Purchase: payment is processed by Stripe — we never see or store your card details

We do not collect analytics, track behaviour, or serve ads.

How we use it

  • To power your Earnings and Appointments rooms
  • To sync your data across your devices via Firebase
  • To process your one-time purchase via Stripe
  • To let clients book appointments through your personal booking link

We do not sell, share, or use your data for marketing.

Third-party services

  • Firebase / Google — authentication and cloud data storage. Firebase Privacy
  • Stripe — one-time payment processing. Stripe Privacy

Data storage & security

Your salon data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, protected by Firebase security rules. Preferences are stored locally on your device and never leave it. We use HTTPS for all connections.

Your rights

You can delete your data at any time from within the app (Settings → Start Fresh). To request full account deletion, email us at chabokchamejun@gmail.com and we will remove your data within 30 days.

Children

Your Salon is intended for professional use by adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes we will update the date below. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
