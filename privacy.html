Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026  ·  Your Salon (CommissionPro & AppointmentPro) by Shahbaz Mirshahi

✓ Your appointment and earnings data stays on your own device. We never see it, never store it on our servers, and never sell or share it. It belongs entirely to you. The one exception is the optional client self-booking feature, where booking requests travel through a secure cloud inbox to reach you — explained in section 3.

1. Who we are

Your Salon — the app suite that includes CommissionPro (Earnings) and AppointmentPro (Appointments) — is operated by Shahbaz Mirshahi ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our website and App at getcommissionpro.com.

2. How your data is stored — on your device

Your Salon is built to be private by design. Everything you enter in the App — client names and cards, appointment dates and times, service charges, tips, prices, inventory, notes, consent records, and your own salon details — is stored only on the device you are using, inside your browser's local storage.

This data is never transmitted to us and is not accessible by us or anyone else. The only information that ever passes through a cloud service is a client booking request you have invited, described in the next section. Because everything else lives on your device, you are always in full control of it.

3. Client self-booking — the one cloud feature (optional)

If you choose to turn on client self-booking and share your booking link, your clients can request an appointment from their own phone without installing anything. To deliver that request to you, the details your client submits — their name, contact number, requested service, date and time, and any notes they add (for example hair details) — travel through Google Firebase (Firestore), a secure cloud database, and land in the request inbox on your device.

  • This information is used only to deliver the booking request to you — never for advertising, profiling, or resale
  • Self-booking is off by default; nothing travels through the cloud unless you enable it and share your link
  • Confirmation texts you send to clients go from your own phone's Messages app — we never see or send them
  • Clients may ask you, the professional, to delete their request or details at any time

Firebase is operated by Google and has its own privacy policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

4. Information we receive when you purchase

When you buy CommissionPro, your payment is processed by Stripe. To complete and support your purchase, we may receive from Stripe:

  • Your name (as entered at checkout)
  • Your email address (for your receipt and access details)

We never see or store your payment card details — these are handled entirely by Stripe.

5. What we do not collect

  • Your appointment, client, or financial data (this stays on your device)
  • Payment card details (handled by Stripe)
  • Your location
  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Browsing history or analytics about how you use the App

6. How we use the information we receive

We use the limited purchase information above only to:

  • Process and confirm your purchase
  • Send you your receipt and access details
  • Provide customer support if you contact us
  • Send rare, important updates about the App

We will never use your information for advertising, or share it with third parties for commercial purposes.

7. Your data, your control

Because your data is stored on your own device, you can manage it directly at any time:

  • Back up / export — use the in-app "Save a copy of my data" feature at any time
  • Delete — clear the App's data from your device through the app or your browser settings; once deleted it is gone, as we hold no copy
  • Move — copy your data to another of your devices using Save / Load

For questions about purchase information we may hold (such as your receipt email), contact us at shahbazmirshahi@mac.com.

8. Third-party services

CommissionPro relies on the following services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Stripe — payment processing: stripe.com/privacy
  • Google Firebase — delivering client self-booking requests (only if you enable it): firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting: github.com/privacy

The App also loads fonts and icons from public content delivery networks (Google Fonts and jsDelivr) purely to display correctly. These do not receive your appointment data.

9. Children's privacy

CommissionPro is intended for professional use by adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect the latest update. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
