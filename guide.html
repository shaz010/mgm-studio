Colour Wheel

↑ Tap any unwanted tone — see what cancels it

1
Open the colour wheel in Appointments
Tap the colour palette icon in your toolbar. The full professional wheel opens instantly.
2
Tap the tone you're seeing in the hair
Brassiness? Orange? Green from a pool? Tap it. The complementary colour lights up directly opposite — that's your neutraliser.
3
Apply with confidence
Use the complementary tone in your formula. No trial and error. No expensive re-do. The colour science is built in.
The most under-used tool in the salon

Most stylists only consult the colour wheel mentally — and that knowledge gaps with time. Having it in your pocket during every consultation means you never have to guess.

↑ Stock bars update as you use products. Red = reorder

1
Add your products
Inventory tab → +. Enter the name, qty on hand, minimum level, and unit. Takes 30 seconds per product.
2
Log usage after each service
Tap the product and enter how much you used. Stock updates instantly — no counting shelves at the end of the week.
3
Spot low stock instantly
Items below your set minimum turn red and flag for reorder. You see it before you run out — not in the middle of a service.
Always with you

Whether you work in one salon or move between locations, your stock levels live in the cloud. Check before a shift, reorder on the go.
