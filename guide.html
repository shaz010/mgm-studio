Section 01
Your money — accurate, every time

Your Salon calculates your take-home the moment you log a booking. No mental maths, no spreadsheets, no end-of-week guessing. Your split is set once and applied automatically to every single appointment.

1
Set your commission rate
Go to Settings → Commission %. Enter the exact percentage your salon pays you — 60%, 55%, 45%, whatever your agreement. Every calculation from that point on uses your real number.
2
Log a booking with price
Add a client, pick a service, enter the charge. That's it. Your Salon instantly shows you the salon cut, your cut, and your take-home — before the client even sits down.
3
Add the tip
Tips are 100% yours — they are never split with the salon. Log them separately in the booking and they are added on top of your commission, not blended into it.
4
Check your Earnings room
Switch to Earnings at the top. See total charged, salon's share, your share, tips, and take-home across any time period — today, this week, this month, or all time.
Cloud safe

Your bookings are stored securely in the cloud. Lose your phone, break your tablet, switch devices — log back in and every appointment, every figure is exactly where you left it.

One price. Yours forever.

No subscription. No monthly fee. No feature unlocks. Pay once and Your Salon is yours — every update, every new feature, at no extra cost.

Section 02
Clients book themselves — no app required

Your clients do not need to download anything. No third-party app, no account, no friction. You share one link — they tap it, pick a time, and the request lands in your inbox for approval.

1
Find your booking link
In Appointments → Settings, tap "My Booking Link." Your personal link is ready the moment you sign up — no setup required.
2
Share it anywhere
Put it in your Instagram bio, text it to clients, add it to your email signature. One link works everywhere — it opens in any browser on any phone.
3
Approve requests
A notification badge appears the moment a client submits a booking. Open it, review the request, and tap Approve or Decline. Approved bookings drop straight into your calendar.
Your client's experience

They tap your link, see your available services and prices, pick a date and time, enter their name and phone number — done. No login, no app download, no hassle. Just a booking.

Section 03
Hair Passport — your client's complete history

Every client gets a digital profile that travels with you. Every appointment, every service, every note — in one place. A new client today has a full history by next visit.

1
Clients are created automatically
The moment you save a booking with a name and phone number, a client card is created. You never need to manage a separate contact list — it builds itself.
2
View full history
Tap any client in the Clients tab to see every appointment they have ever had with you — service, price, date, notes. Instantly recall what you did last time without asking.
3
Add notes that matter
Record anything important on a booking — formula used, allergies, preferences, what they asked for next time. It stays with that appointment permanently.
Why it builds trust

Remembering a client's history without asking feels like magic to them. That personal touch — "last time we used 7N with a splash of 9G" — is the difference between a client who comes back and one who doesn't.

Section 04
Colour wheel — professional correction in your pocket

The colour wheel is one of the most powerful tools in a colourist's kit — and one of the most under-used. Years of training distilled into a tap. No trichology textbook, no guesswork, no waiting until you remember.

1
Open the colour wheel
Tap the colour palette icon in your toolbar. The full professional colour wheel opens — primary, secondary, and tertiary colours with their relationships mapped visually.
2
Identify the unwanted tone
Tap the colour you are seeing in the hair — brassiness, orange, red, green. The wheel instantly shows you the exact neutralising colour directly opposite it.
3
Apply with confidence
Use the complementary colour to neutralise the unwanted tone. No more trial and error. What takes years of instinct to develop is now a two-second reference check.
Why this matters

Colour correction mistakes are expensive — in product, in time, and in client confidence. Having the colour wheel in your pocket during a consultation or correction is the same as having your most experienced mentor standing next to you.

Section 05
Inventory — always know what you have

Running out of a product mid-service is one of the most avoidable frustrations in the salon. Your Salon tracks everything you carry — stock levels, low-stock alerts, and usage history — always on you.

1
Add your products
Go to the Inventory tab. Tap the + button and add each product — name, quantity on hand, the minimum level before you get a low-stock alert, and the unit (tubes, bottles, grams).
2
Mark usage after each service
After a colour service, tap the product and log how much you used. Your remaining stock updates instantly. No counting shelves at the end of the week.
3
Spot low stock at a glance
Items below your minimum level are flagged. You see exactly what needs reordering before you run out — not after.
Always with you

Whether you work in one salon or move between locations, your inventory data lives in the cloud. Check stock levels from anywhere — before a shift, between clients, or while placing an order.

Section 06
Reports, search & tax — your business at a glance

Every appointment you log builds a searchable, filterable record of your business. When tax season arrives, or when you need to understand a slow week, the answers are already there.

1
Search any client or service
Tap the search icon in Appointments. Type a client name, a service, or a date. Results appear instantly across your entire history — no filter menus, no date ranges to set.
2
View earnings by period
In the Earnings room, switch between Today, This Week, This Month, and All Time. Your totals recalculate instantly. Compare periods to understand your busiest times and highest-earning services.
3
Tax time — when you choose
Your complete income record is always there. When you're ready to prepare for tax, every charge, every tip, and every date is logged and filterable. Hand your accountant a complete picture in minutes, not days.
Your decision, your timing

Tax reporting is a capability, not a requirement. Use it when it suits you. The data is always there when you need it — you are never locked into a particular workflow.

Section 09
Install it like a native app — no App Store

Your Salon installs straight from your browser. No App Store, no lengthy download, no account required to install. Two taps and it lives on your home screen like any other app.

1
Open in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android)
Navigate to getcommissionpro.com/salon.html in your mobile browser.
2
Tap Share → Add to Home Screen
On iPhone: tap the Share button (the box with an arrow) at the bottom of Safari, then scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen." On Android: tap the three-dot menu → "Add to Home Screen."
3
Open from your home screen
Tap the ✨ Your Salon icon. It opens full-screen, with no browser bar — exactly like a native app. Works offline for core features once installed.
Why no App Store?

App stores add friction — for you and your clients. Your Salon installs instantly, updates silently in the background, and never asks you to "update to the latest version." It is always current the next time you open it.
